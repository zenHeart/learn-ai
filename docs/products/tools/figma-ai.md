# AI for Designers (Figma)

Designers are not being replaced, but their workflows are speeding up 10x.

## Figma Native AI

Figma is rolling out native AI features:
1.  **Search**: "Find the login screen" search within your canvas.
2.  **Layer Renaming**: Auto-rename "Frame 1232" to "Submit Button".
3.  **Content Population**: Auto-fill cards with realistic names/photos instead of "Lorem Ipsum".

## Top Plugins

### 1. Relume Library
Generates wireframes and site maps instantly.
- **Workflow**: Describe sitemap -> Export to Figma -> Export to Webflow/React.

### 2. Magician (by Diagram)
- **Text to Icon**: "A vector icon of a robot cat".
- **Copywriting**: "Rewrite this headline to be punchier".

### 3. Frontitude
AI copy management directly in Figma.

## Design Workflow 2.0

1.  **Ideation**: Midjourney for moodboards.
2.  **Wireframe**: Relume/Figma AI.
3.  **High Fidelity**: Human designer polishes.
4.  **Handoff**: Figma Dev Mode (with AI explanations).
